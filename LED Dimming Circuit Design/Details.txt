Title : Sterling Light Dimming Circuit
Date : 2018.4
Skills: Analog Circuit design, Power Circuit design, PWM buck-boost power configuration, Current Sensing and feedback Control, PCB design,

Desc: It is driver circuit for LEDs and controlled by HV9963 from MicroChip. It is driven by current constant control so we can connect any counts LEDs in serial.
        And it has some feedback and user control logic by circuit potentiometer and protection unit.
